How It Works

How to Report an FIR

  1. Click on the "Report Crime Now" button on the homepage.
  2. Fill in the required details in the FIR form.
  3. Submit the form and receive a confirmation with your FIR number.

How to Use the Crime Hot Map

  1. Navigate to the "View Crime Hotspots" section on the homepage.
  2. Interact with the map to view crime density in different areas.
  3. Click on a hotspot for more details about reported crimes.

How to Track FIR Status

  1. Log in to your account on the website.
  2. Go to the "Track FIR Status" section.
  3. Enter your FIR number to view the current status.

FAQs

How long does it take to file an FIR?

Filing an FIR typically takes less than 10 minutes if you have all the necessary details.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption to ensure your data is safe and secure.

Can I report a crime anonymously?

Yes, you can choose to report a crime anonymously without providing personal details.

What happens after I submit an FIR?

Your FIR is reviewed by the authorities, and you will receive updates on the status via email or SMS.
